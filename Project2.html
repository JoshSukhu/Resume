Summary

In this project, we worked on the cisco paket tracer software to simulate real world connections and setups. This project's purpose was to create a router,
pc LAN setup in which every PC connected on the LAN would only be able to connect to eachother.
These devices were not allowed to connect to the internet, just to eachother, much like the setup of many office buildings.

Skills Learned

  • Cisco packet tracer
  • Network fundamentals(ie. understanding of devices, cofiguring ip addresses, static/dhcp, exploring LAN, WAN,WLAN)
  • Problem-Solving, Critical Thinking, Adaptability
